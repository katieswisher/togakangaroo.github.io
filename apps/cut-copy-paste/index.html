Programmers spend a lot of time typing. Many hours a day in fact. Saving a few seconds by learning a shortcut for something you do often will pay you back almost immediately.

Three of the most helpful shortcuts that you will use all of the time are

Copy:
ctrl+c
Cut:
ctrl+x
Paste:
ctrl+p

Practice these commands by making the text on the left match the text on the right. You may also ctrl+z (undo), ctrl+shift+z (redo), and use the enter key.

Congratulations. You Win!
